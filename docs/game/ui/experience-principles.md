# Experience principles

1. **Enter quickly.** Once session and character state are ready, a returning player starts with one primary action.
2. **Keep context.** Authentication, settings, and brief supporting content open above Home without discarding the pending play choice.
3. **Protect the play space.** HUD modules stay near edges or actors and avoid the local player, aim direction, and threats.
4. **Show actionable state.** Prioritize health, resources, cooldowns, carried materials, danger, squad state, and interaction progress. Reference detail belongs in the menu.
5. **Make rules visible.** Boundaries, loadout locks, loot eligibility, and death consequences must not depend on prior knowledge.
6. **Preserve skill and readability.** UI cannot hide telegraphs, misrepresent hitboxes, choose tactical answers, or reveal forbidden information. It follows the design's [visual channels](../design/visual-direction.md#readability-system).
7. **Support every target size.** Desktop and mobile expose the same essential state and actions with appropriate layouts and controls.
